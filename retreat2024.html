The first SN group retreat

We will meet in Vallgorguina (Vallès Oriental) for three days to finish up a couple of on-going but in an advance stage projects. We will also have a few short hands-on sessions on tools group members have either developed or are using for their research, and we will have time for a couple of Walking-Working-Excursions (WWE).
